## Project Overview: Homology Modeling of TGB2

### **Bioinformatics Toolset**
* **Structure Prediction:** Swiss-Model, I-TASSER, and Robetta.
* **Structure Visualization & Editing:** UCSF Chimera and WinCoot.
* **Refinement & Optimization:** GalaxyRefine.
* **Validation Suites:** ERRAT, Verify 3D, PROCHECK, and MolProbity.

---

### **Results Summary**
* **Target Protein:** Movement protein TGB2 of Wheat virus Q (UniProt ID: A0A8D5WHE7).
* **Initial Challenge:** The protein lacked a resolved PDB structure; initial automated predictions from I-TASSER and Swiss-Model contained significant geometric outliers and formatting errors.
* **Final Model Selection:** A high-confidence model was derived from Robetta and further optimized through iterative refinement.
* **Geometric Quality:** Achieved a final Ramachandran favored score of 94.07%.
* **Structural Integrity:** Refinement successfully eliminated all geometric anomalies, resulting in 0 outliers, 0 poor rotamers, and a 99% favored rotamer score.
